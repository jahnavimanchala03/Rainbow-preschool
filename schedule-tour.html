Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical tour last?

Our tours typically last around 30-45 minutes, depending on your questions and interests. We'll make sure you have time to see all areas of our facility and get all your questions answered.

Should I bring my child to the tour?

You're welcome to bring your child along! It can be helpful for them to see the environment and meet some of the teachers. However, if you prefer to tour first on your own, that's perfectly fine too.

What should I expect during the tour?

During your tour, you'll be guided by a member of our administrative team who will show you our classrooms, outdoor play areas, and other facilities. You'll learn about our curriculum, daily schedules, and have the opportunity to meet teachers and observe classroom activities if you visit during program hours.

Can I schedule a tour outside of the available times?

We understand that schedules can be challenging. If none of our listed times work for you, please contact us directly at (505) 123-4567, and we'll do our best to accommodate your schedule.

Is there any information I should bring to the tour?

There's no need to bring any specific documents for the tour. If you're interested in enrolling after the tour, we can provide you with all the necessary enrollment forms and information at that time.
