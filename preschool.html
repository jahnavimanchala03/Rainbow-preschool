Preschool Program

Building foundations for lifelong learning for children ages 3-4 years

About Our Preschool Program

Our Preschool Program at Rainbow Pre School is designed for children ages 3-4 years, focusing on creating a balanced approach to learning through structured activities and creative play. During these formative years, children develop crucial pre-academic skills while nurturing their natural curiosity and love of discovery.

Our preschool classrooms are engaging environments where children can explore language, mathematics, science, and the arts through hands-on activities and guided learning experiences. Our teachers are trained to recognize each child's individual learning style and interests, adapting activities to support their unique developmental journey.

Early Literacy

Our preschool program builds strong foundations for reading and writing through daily exposure to books, storytelling, and print materials. Children develop letter recognition, phonological awareness, and vocabulary skills through engaging activities and interactive read-alouds.

Early Mathematics

Children develop foundational math concepts through hands-on experiences with counting, sorting, patterning, and basic measurement. Our approach emphasizes mathematical thinking in everyday contexts, helping children recognize numbers and numerical relationships in their environment.

Social-Emotional Development

Our program fosters emotional intelligence, self-regulation, and positive social interactions. Children learn to identify and express their feelings, develop empathy, collaborate with peers, and build confidence in their abilities.

Science and Discovery

Through observation, experimentation, and exploration, preschoolers develop scientific thinking skills. Our curriculum includes nature studies, simple science experiments, and opportunities to ask questions and seek answers about the world around them.

Creative Arts

Children express themselves through various art forms, including painting, drawing, music, movement, and dramatic play. These activities nurture creativity, fine motor skills, and self-expression while introducing elements of color, shape, rhythm, and design.

Program Details

  • Ages: 3-4 years
  • Student-to-teacher ratio: 8:1
  • Full-time and part-time options
  • Nutritious meals and snacks provided

Daily Schedule

Our preschool day includes:

  • Morning circle time
  • Small group activities
  • Center-based learning
  • Outdoor exploration
  • Art and music
  • Story time
  • Rest/quiet time
