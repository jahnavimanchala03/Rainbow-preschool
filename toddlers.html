Toddler Program

Nurturing growth and discovery for children ages 18-36 months

About Our Toddler Program

Our Toddler Program at Rainbow Pre School is designed to create a warm, responsive environment where your young child can explore and develop confidence. We understand that toddlers are in a critical period of development, with rapidly expanding language skills, growing independence, and curiosity about the world around them.

Our experienced teachers provide attentive care and guidance, creating a safe space for toddlers to explore their environment, develop social skills, and build a foundation for future learning. We maintain low teacher-to-child ratios to ensure each child receives the individual attention they need.

Language Development

We support your toddler's emerging language skills through stories, songs, and continuous conversation throughout the day. Our classrooms are rich with print materials, pictures, and labels to help children connect words with objects and ideas.

Sensory Exploration

Toddlers learn best through their senses. Our classrooms offer varied sensory experiences including water play, sand tables, textured materials, and art projects that encourage exploration through touch, sight, sound, and movement.

Social Development

We guide toddlers in developing positive relationships with peers and teachers. Children learn to take turns, share, and express their feelings appropriately in a supportive environment.

Physical Development

Our program includes both fine and gross motor activities to support physical development. Toddlers engage in activities like stacking blocks, using puzzles, climbing, dancing, and outdoor play to build strength, coordination, and body awareness.

Cognitive Skills

Through age-appropriate activities and play, we introduce basic concepts such as colors, shapes, counting, and cause-and-effect relationships. Our curriculum encourages problem-solving and critical thinking in ways that engage toddlers' natural curiosity.

Program Details

  • Ages: 18-36 months
  • Student-to-teacher ratio: 4:1
  • Full-time and part-time options
  • Nutritious meals and snacks provided

Daily Schedule

Our toddler day includes:

  • Morning circle time
  • Sensory exploration
  • Outdoor play
  • Music and movement
  • Story time
  • Rest/nap time
  • Small group activities
