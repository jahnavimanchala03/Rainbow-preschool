About Rainbow Pre School

At Rainbow Pre School, we believe in creating a colorful and vibrant learning environment that inspires curiosity and creativity in young minds. Established in 2025, our preschool offers care and education for children from 6 months to 5 years old, with a unique blend of play-based learning and structured activities designed to prepare children for kindergarten and beyond.

Our dedicated teachers are passionate about early childhood education and are committed to providing individualized attention to each child. We focus on developing social, emotional, cognitive, and physical skills through engaging activities that make learning fun.

Located in the heart of Albuquerque at 7604 Rio Guadalupe Pl NE, our facility is designed with safety and learning in mind. We invite you to schedule a visit and see why families choose Rainbow Pre School for their children's early education journey.
