Pre-K Program

Preparing for kindergarten success for children ages 4-5 years

About Our Pre-K Program

Our Pre-K Program at Rainbow Pre School is specifically designed to prepare 4-5 year old children for a successful transition to kindergarten. This comprehensive program emphasizes academic readiness, social skills, and independent learning, while maintaining our commitment to developmentally appropriate practices and the joy of learning.

Our Pre-K classrooms are vibrant environments where children engage in more structured learning experiences while still having ample opportunities for creative exploration and play. We focus on building confidence, independence, and a love of learning that will serve children well throughout their educational journey.

Literacy & Language Arts

Our Pre-K program builds strong foundations in reading and writing readiness. Children develop letter recognition, phonological awareness, and early writing skills through engaging activities. They practice storytelling, expand their vocabulary, and develop listening comprehension in preparation for formal reading instruction in kindergarten.

Mathematics

Children develop number sense, counting skills, pattern recognition, measurement concepts, and spatial awareness. Our hands-on approach to mathematics encourages problem-solving and critical thinking as children explore mathematical concepts in meaningful contexts.

Social Studies & Sciences

Our curriculum introduces children to their community, diverse cultures, and the natural world. Through hands-on experiments, nature exploration, and community awareness activities, children develop observation skills, scientific inquiry, and an understanding of their place in the world.

Social-Emotional Development

We emphasize self-regulation, problem-solving, cooperation, and responsibility. Children learn to express their feelings appropriately, resolve conflicts peacefully, and develop the interpersonal skills needed for success in a kindergarten classroom environment.

Independence & Executive Function

Our Pre-K program promotes self-help skills, time management, following multi-step directions, and task completion. These executive function skills are crucial for kindergarten readiness and lifelong learning success.

Program Details

  • Ages: 4-5 years
  • Student-to-teacher ratio: 10:1
  • Full-time and part-time options
  • Nutritious meals and snacks provided

Daily Schedule

Our Pre-K day includes:

  • Morning meeting and calendar
  • Small group instruction
  • Learning centers exploration
  • Outdoor physical activity
  • STEAM activities
  • Rest/quiet time
  • Literacy and numeracy
